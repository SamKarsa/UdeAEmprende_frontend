About us

What are we?

UdeA emprende is a comunity project to help the people who have a entrepreneurship, business or enterprise inside of we Alma Máter Universidad de Antioquia.

What you can do in this web?

In this website you can from create a enterprise, share or simply navegative through the web page to find anything type of a entepreneurship you want, separate by categories like food, cosmetics, entertainment, sports and a lot more..

History

UdeA beginnings

UdeA emprende was created in 2025 initally for a final project for Talento Tech Programming Bootcamp but this web page became a real web page for the Universidad de Antioquia comunity, many people by day use this web page to interact with the personal entrepreneurships in the Alma Máter.
