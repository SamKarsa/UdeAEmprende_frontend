About UdeA Emprende

Promote entrepreneurship in our university community.

OUR MISSION

Who are we?

UdeA Emprende is a community initiative created to support entrepreneurs, businesses and companies within our Alma Mater, the University of Antioquia. We seek to create an ecosystem where innovative ideas can grow and prosper, connecting talent, resources and opportunities.

SERVICES

What can you do on this website?

On this platform you can create your own company, share your venture or simply browse to discover interesting projects classified by categories such as food, cosmetics, entertainment, sports and many more.

OUR JOURNEY

Our History

From project to reality

UdeA Emprende was born in 2025 as the final project of the Talento Tech Programming Bootcamp, but quickly evolved into a real platform for the entire community of the University of Antioquia. Currently, numerous students, graduates and professors use this space to interact with personal ventures within the Alma Mater, creating an ecosystem of innovation and collaboration.
